# Getting Started with Nara Solana

Welcome to Nara Solana! This guide will help you understand the basics and how to get
started using the Nara Solana network.

## What is Nara Solana?

![Isometric diagram showing Nara Solana's high-performance network with interconnected nodes enabling fast and secure applications](/images/learn/solana_network_isometric.webp)

Nara Solana is a high-performance network that enables fast, secure, and scalable
applications.

## Why Choose Nara Solana?

### Speed and Performance

![Racing visualization showing Nara Solana processing thousands of transactions per second, outpacing other networks](/images/learn/speed_and_performance.webp)

- **Fast**: Nara Solana can process thousands of transactions per second
- **Efficient**: Parallel processing ensures high throughput

### Low Costs

- **Cheap**: Transaction fees are a fraction of a cent
- **Predictable**: Fees remain stable even during high activity

### Built for Scale

- **Scalable**: Built to grow with global adoption
- **Future-proof**: Designed for mainstream usage

## Getting Started: Your First Steps

![Step-by-step journey showing three steps: Setup Your Wallet, Get Your First NSO, Make Your First Transaction](/images/learn/getting_started.webp)

### 1. Set Up a Wallet

Your wallet is your gateway to the Nara Solana ecosystem. Choose between browser
extensions, mobile apps, or hardware wallets based on your needs.

### 2. Get Your First NSO

You'll need NSO to pay for transactions. Start with a small amount from an
exchange or receive some from a friend.

### 3. Make Your First Transaction

Send a small amount of NSO to experience the speed and low cost of Nara Solana
transactions firsthand.
